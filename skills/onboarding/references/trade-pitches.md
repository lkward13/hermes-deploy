# Trade-matched fallback pitches

Use these when `opener.json` is missing or thin, but you know the owner's trade (from the trade tap or the app). Pick the lead concept for the trade, name the provider you want them to connect, and promise the first move. Real numbers come from `headline_data` once connected; until then, tease with a believable typical, never a fake specific.

Zero em dashes anywhere.

## Contractor / trades

> "Connect QuickBooks and my first move is hunting down every job you finished but never invoiced, plus everyone who owes you and is past due. For most contractors that's a few thousand bucks just sitting there. Want me to go look?" [Connect QuickBooks]

If they use JobNimbus or Jobber instead, swap the provider: "Connect JobNimbus and I'll find every completed job that never got billed."

## Home services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing)

> "Hook up QuickBooks (or Jobber) and the first thing I do is tell you who's past due and text them a pay link. Then I watch for late payers from here on so you never chase one by hand again." [Connect QuickBooks] [Connect Jobber]

## Realtor / brokerage

Realtors get a live-lead demo, not invoices.

> "Connect BoldTrail (or GoHighLevel) and I'll show you which leads are looking at listings right now and haven't heard back from you. Those are the ones about to call someone else." [Connect BoldTrail]

## Retail / shop

> "Connect Clover and I'll tell you how today stacks up against a normal day and what your best sellers are. Next time the register rings I'm already tracking it." [Connect Clover]

## Something else / unknown

Default to the money pitch, it travels:

> "Connect QuickBooks and the first thing I'll do is tell you exactly who owes you and text the late ones a pay link. Want me to take a look at your books?" [Connect QuickBooks]

## Picking the provider

Lead with the highest-charge provider the trade is likely to have:

| Trade | First provider | Lead concept |
|---|---|---|
| Contractor / trades | QuickBooks, JobNimbus, Jobber | uninvoiced jobs + overdue AR |
| Home services | QuickBooks, Jobber | overdue AR + pay links |
| Realtor | BoldTrail, GoHighLevel | hot leads going cold |
| Retail | Clover | today vs typical + top seller |
| Unknown | QuickBooks | who owes you |
